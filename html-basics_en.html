Text Level Semantics

Element Purpose Example of Use
abbr Abbreviation, acronym

Many applications are based on HTTP.
b Text to be stylistically offset from the normal prose without conveying any extra importance (e.g., a keyword or a product name).

Our company sells Nimbus 2000 broomsticks.
cite Title of a work.

My favourite book by the author is Foucault's Pendulum.
code Fragment of computer code.

See the currentTimeMillis() method of the java.lang.System class.

data Content with a machine-readable form.

two thousand forty-eight
dfn A term being defined.

Markup languages are computer languages for annotating text.
em Text that has stress emphasis.

You must leave immediately.
i Text in an alternate voice or mood, or otherwise offset from the normal prose (e.g., a phrase in another language).

The movie begins in medias res: the protagonist is running away from a bunch of bloodthirsty vampires.
kbd User input

Press Ctrl+Q to exit the program.
mark Marked or highlighted text.

Better late than never.
meter A scalar value within a known range.

Your score is 30/40s.
q Short inline quotation. Do. Or do not. There is no try.
s No longer accurate or no longer relevant information.

Cheap brooms! Sold out!
samp Output from a computer program.

The program prints the text Hello, World! to the console.
small Small print text There is no warranty that the information provided here is accurate.
strong Text with strong importance. Beware of the dog!
sub Subscript

Overconsumption of H2O can be dangerous.
sup Superscript

JavaTM
time Date, time

Tardar Sauce (commonly known as Grumpy Cat) was born on April 4, 2012.
u Text with an unarticulated, though explicitly rendered, non-textual annotation (e.g., labeling the text as being misspelt).

Romanes ite domus (See: Romani ite domum)
var Variable

The eps variable specifies the desired accuracy.

extern double eps;

Verbatim Code

The '<' and the '&' characters require special care in text!

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html lang="en">
    <head>
        <meta charset="UTF-8">
        <title>Hello, World!</title>
    </head>
    <body>
        <p>Hello, World!</p>
    </body>
</html>
